Privacy Policy

General
We do not share any user data with third parties.
Our application does not persistently store any sensitive user data. We use temporary session tokens for authentication purposes, which are securely managed and automatically expire after use. All data, including these temporary tokens, is encrypted in transit using industry-standard protocols to ensure secure transmission.
Users can revoke our application's access to their YouTube/Spotify data at any time through their Google/Spotify account settings.
Youtube/Google
By logging into our application using your Google account, you grant us limited access to your YouTube data only. We do not have access to any other Google services or personal information outside of YouTube-specific data.
Specifically, we use this access to modify the YouTube playlists that you explicitly choose to share with our application. We cannot access your personal information or any data from other Google services.
Spotify
By connecting your Spotify account to our application, you grant us limited access to your Spotify data. We only access the following information:
Your Spotify username, user_id, email, profile picture, your playlists (names and contents). Again, our application doesn't story any sensitive user data.
Specifically, we use this to access and modify your Spotify playlists that you explicitly choose to share with our application.
